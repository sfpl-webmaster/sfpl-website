About us – Sangam Forgings

Open die forging company, Sangam Forgings Pvt Ltd, was started in 1980 to cater to critical sectors of the economy like Steel Plants, Railways, Power Plants, Mining, Cement, Material Handling, Hydrocarbons, Defence, Sugar, Road making and Construction. Centrally located in the industrial city of Bhilai, Chhattisgarh, Sangam Forgings is well connected through Road and Rail network to all parts of the country.

SFPL has played a vital role in the growth of hundreds of industries across India and the world and has evolved to become one of the leading manufacturers of high quality ferrous forgings in India today.

As an ISO 9001-2015 certified company, excellence is expected. When you place an order with Sangam Forgings Pvt Ltd, you will receive a forging that will stand up in service from people that stand behind their product. Continuing education and innovation in all aspects of manufacturing sets us apart.

With over 80,000 sq. feet under roof, SFPL produces open & close die forgings with carbon steel, alloy steel and other special steel grades.  SFPL can produce shapes that include: shafts, axles, wheels, rings, blocks, bars and other custom shapes. In addition to forging, value added secondary processing operations include: heat treatment, rough machining, saw cutting, hole boring, destructive and non destructive testing. Finish machining is also available.

Since 1980, Sangam Forgings has supplied the following markets with quality forgings:

  • Steel
  • Heavy Engineering
  • Mining
  • Power Generation
  • Gear Box Manufacturers
  • Oil & Gas
  • Cement
  • Material Handling
  • Ship Building
  • Rolling Mills

With an annual production capacity of:
* 12000 MT in Ope Die Forgings
* 1000 MT in Close Die Forgings (including specialty fasteners)

we can cater to all your Custom High-Quality Forging Requirements “On Time, Every Time“
