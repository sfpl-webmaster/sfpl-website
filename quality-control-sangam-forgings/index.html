Quality Control – Sangam Forgings

Our comprehensive 7 - stage Quality Control process ensures a High Quality Forging every time. We provide test certificates from our own lab / government approved lab as per client specifications.

Stage 1: Material Check

  • Raw Material Inspection

  • Visual Inspection

  • Chemical Analysis Check

Stage 2: Cutting

  • Weight Measurement

  • Cut Surface Check

  • Marking for Identification

Stage 3: Heating

  • Temperature Check

Stage 4: Forging & Normalizing

  • Visual Check

  • Temperature Check

  • Dimensional Check

  • Weight Measurement

Stage 5: Heat Treatment

  • Visual Check

  • Temperature Check

  • Soaking Time

  • Cooling Time

  • Hardness Check

Stage 6: Machining

  • Visual Check

  • Dimensional Check

Stage 7: Final Inspection

  • Dye Penetrant Testing

  • Magnetic Particle Testing

  • Ultrasonic Testing

  • Tensile Testing

  • Impact Testing

  • Microscopic Testing

  • Marking Check

  • Dimensional Check

  • Weight Measurement

  • Packing Inspection
