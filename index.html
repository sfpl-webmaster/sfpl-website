Sangam Forgings Private Limited (India) represents the best in custom high quality open – die forgings. Open die forging involves shaping of metals by heating and compressing between two dies, without fully enclosing the work piece. Our open die forging service covers a variety of forged shapes and forging materials, such as carbon steel, various grades of alloy steel and other special steels. SFPL can work open – die forgings into wheels, axles, sleeves, shafts, gear blanks, rings, blocks, bars as well as other custom shapes and also provide complete heat treatment and finish machining solutions as well. Open – die forgings manufactured by us feature – improved strength, continuous grain flow, better fatigue resistance and more, hence, we always confidently commit that –
