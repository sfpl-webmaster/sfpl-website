ABOUT US

Sangam Forgings Private Limited, a part of Simplex Group of Industries, was established in 1980 to cater to critical sectors of the economy like steel, heavy engineering, power generation (thermal, hydro and nuclear), oil and gas, cement, material handling, shipbuilding, aerospace and defence.

Today it evolved to become one of the leading producers of high quality ferrous forgings in India.
